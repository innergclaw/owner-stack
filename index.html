Full Stack Details

Build the five pieces in the right order.

The goal is simple: stop sending attention into random inboxes and dead links. Build a system that captures, sells, and follows up every time.

01

Logo + Identity

Your first trust signal.

  • Clean logo that reads fast on phone screens.
  • Two to four brand colors that stay consistent everywhere.
  • Simple type system for flyers, website, invoices, and posts.
Build next: one primary logo, one icon mark, one brand sheet.
02

Domain + Website

Your owned home base.

  • Short domain people can remember, say, and type.
  • Homepage that says what you do, who it is for, and what to do next.
  • Service page or offer block that turns interest into action.
Build next: domain, homepage, offer section, contact route.
03

Lead Form

The bridge from viewer to prospect.

  • Ask for only what you need: name, email, and one intent question.
  • Send every lead into one place so follow-up is never lost.
  • Trigger a thank-you message or next-step email immediately.
Build next: embedded form, lead storage, confirmation message.
04

Payment Flow

The path from yes to paid.

  • Payment link for deposits, consults, products, or starter offers.
  • Clear receipt and onboarding instructions after payment.
  • One simple checkout route before building complicated systems.
Build next: payment link, checkout page, post-payment email.
05

Email List

The relationship you own.

  • Welcome email that reminds people why they signed up.
  • Weekly value email that builds trust before the sale.
  • Offer email that moves warm attention into a real next step.
Build next: welcome email, tags, first 3-message sequence.
